IIITG students come from across India, representing diverse perspectives, experiences, backgrounds and cultures. Once here, IIITG students discover extraordinary freedom of opportunity -- to explore, to collaborate and to challenge themselves.

Undergraduate

IIITG attracts, challenges, and cultivates independent thinkers with the power and drive to create personally meaningful lives. IIITG offers the following undergraduate programmes - a) B.Tech. in Compute Science and Engineering (CSE) b) B.Tech. in Electronic s and Communication Engineering (ECE)
